Instagram Growth Engine

A multi-pronged approach to transform our Instagram profile into a community hub and a powerful sales channel.

Pillar 1: A Feast for the Eyes

Our content will focus on high-quality, visually stunning media that stops the scroll. We will follow a content pillar strategy to ensure variety and consistency.

  • Delectable Food & Drinks: Close-ups, flat lays, and latte art videos that make followers hungry.
  • Vibrant Ambiance: Showcase our cozy corners, decor, and the general vibe of the cafe.
  • Behind-the-Scenes: Reels showing baristas at work, ingredients being prepped, or new recipes being tested.
  • User-Generated Content (UGC): Reposting the best customer photos and stories to build community.
  • Promotions & Offers: Clear, attractive graphics for our special deals.

Pillar 2: Building a Community

We will actively engage with our audience to build relationships, not just collect followers. This creates loyalty and turns followers into advocates.

  • Run a monthly photo contest: "Share your best #JumpingBeansMoment to win a free meal."
  • Use interactive Story stickers: Polls, quizzes, and question boxes to spark conversations.
  • Respond to all comments and DMs within a few hours to show we're listening.
  • Engage with posts from other local businesses and customers who tag us.

Pillar 3: Leveraging Local Voices

Collaborating with Colombo-based food bloggers and influencers will introduce our cafe to thousands of potential new customers in our target demographic.

  • Identify 5-10 micro-influencers (1k-10k followers) with high engagement rates.
  • Offer a complimentary meal in exchange for a dedicated post and a series of stories.
  • Provide a unique discount code for their followers to track conversions.
  • Build long-term relationships with influencers who genuinely love our brand for ongoing partnerships.
