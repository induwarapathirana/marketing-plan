Grand Strategy

A strategic overview of our market position, target audience, and primary objectives for a successful rebrand and growth period.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths: Prime location with high, concentrated foot traffic (students/corporates).
Weaknesses: Near-zero social media presence (48 followers), no brand equity for "The Bean Bar," and a very limited ad budget (LKR 20k).
Opportunities: Rebrand allows for a fresh, high-impact launch. Hyper-local digital ads can be highly effective. Strong potential to build loyalty in a captive audience.
Threats: Strong, established on-site competitors (Barista, Brown Bean). Existing customer habits are hard to break.

The Phased Roadmap

A detailed, month-by-month plan to transition from "Jumping Beans" to "The Bean Bar," starting now.

Focus: Awaken the Account & Establish Consistency

Goal is to clean up the existing 'Jumping Beans' page and start posting high-quality content regularly. No ad spend this month.

  • Profile Cleanup: Archive all old, low-quality posts for a fresh start. Update the bio to be more engaging.
  • Consistent Posting: Post 3 times a week (Mon, Wed, Fri) featuring your best current drinks and food.
  • Engage with Stories: Use polls and questions ("Espresso or Latte?") to encourage interaction.
  • Local Hashtag Research: Identify and start using relevant local hashtags (#ColomboFoodie, #WSO2Life, #CafeHoppingLK).

Focus: Initial Growth & Begin the Teaser Campaign

We'll invest a small amount in ads to grow our initial audience and start hinting that a change is coming.

  • Launch Teaser Content: Late in the month, post cryptic stories/posts: "Something new is brewing..." or "2026 is going to be a fresh start."
  • Run Engagement Ads (LKR 5,000): Promote your best-performing posts to a targeted audience (Age 18-45, within 2km of WSO2) to gain new followers.
  • Highlight Ambiance: Post photos and Reels of the cafe space, focusing on its appeal as a work/study spot.

Focus: Build Maximum Anticipation

This month is all about creating hype for the January 1st reveal. We ramp up ad spend significantly.

  • Daily Countdown: In the last week, use stories for a daily countdown to the rebrand.
  • Behind-the-Scenes Peeks: Show snippets of the new brand (a new cup, a painted wall, a menu font) without revealing the name.
  • Run "Secret's Out Soon" Ads (LKR 15,000): Use video ads to build curiosity, targeting the same local audience with a message of "Your coffee break is getting an upgrade. Jan 1st."
  • Finalize Launch Assets: Prepare all social media posts, in-store signage, and delivery app profiles for "The Bean Bar."

Focus: Execute a Flawless, High-Impact Launch

On January 1st, we go live. The entire LKR 20,000 budget is deployed to drive awareness, foot traffic, and first-time orders.

  • The Great Switch (Jan 1st): Change Instagram handle, archive old posts, and upload a 9-grid of professional "The Bean Bar" content at once.
  • Launch Offers LIVE: Announce compelling launch week offers (e.g., "50% Off Your First Coffee," "Free Brownie with any Lunch Combo").
  • Full-Scale Ad Campaign (LKR 20,000): Run ads promoting the launch offers with aggressive geo-fencing (1km radius). Optimize for reach and store visits.
  • Update Delivery Apps: Rebrand profiles on Uber Eats & PickMe. Feature launch-exclusive deals prominently at the top of the menu.
  • Micro-Influencer Day: Invite 3-5 local student influencers for a free experience in the first week in exchange for posts and stories.
