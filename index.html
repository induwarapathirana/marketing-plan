Strategic Overview

This is our game plan for successfully launching "The Bean Bar" and establishing a strong market presence in the first quarter.

Current Situation (SWOT Analysis)

Understanding our position is key. We have a prime location but face stiff competition and the challenge of a complete rebrand with a low starting follower count.

Strengths

  • Prime location with high foot traffic from corporates & students.
  • Captive audience within the WSO2 building.

Weaknesses

  • Extremely low Instagram following (48).
  • Zero brand recognition for "The Bean Bar".
  • Limited marketing ad budget (LKR 20k/month).

Opportunities

  • Rebranding creates a chance for a fresh, exciting launch.
  • Targeted geo-fenced ads can be highly effective.
  • Potential for strong loyalty from the student/corporate demographic.

Threats

  • Direct, established competition on-site (Barista, Brown Bean).
  • Customer loyalty to existing cafes.
  • Market noise from many Colombo cafes.

The 3-Month Rebranding Roadmap

A phased plan to introduce and grow "The Bean Bar" brand from the ground up.

Focus: Build Anticipation & Prepare Assets

Using the existing 'Jumping Beans' Instagram account, we'll hint at a big change without revealing the new name.

  • Instagram Teaser Campaign: Post stories and reels with captions like "Something new is brewing...", "Getting a fresh start in 2026.", "Your coffee break is getting an upgrade."
  • Finalize 'The Bean Bar' Branding: Complete logo, menu design, packaging, and in-store signage.
  • Content Production: Conduct a photoshoot of the new menu items and cafe ambiance to have a bank of high-quality content ready for launch.
  • Plan Launch Week Offers: Decide on compelling offers (e.g., "First 50 Coffees Free," "25% off all week") to drive initial foot traffic.

Focus: Execute a High-Impact Launch

On January 1st, we flip the switch. The goal is maximum awareness and trial within our 2km radius.

  • Instagram Overhaul (Jan 1st):
    • Change handle to @TheBeanBar.Colombo (or similar).
    • Archive all old 'Jumping Beans' posts.
    • Post 9 new, high-quality images at once to create a full, branded grid.
    • Update bio with new name, location, and a call-to-action for the launch offer.
  • Launch Geo-Targeted Ads: Run Instagram/Facebook ads promoting the launch offer, targeted specifically to people within a 2km radius of the WSO2 building with interests in coffee, universities, and corporate life.
  • In-Store Execution: Display new signage and actively promote launch offers to every customer.
  • Update Delivery Apps: Rebrand the store profile on Uber Eats & PickMe Foods. Run platform-exclusive launch deals.

Focus: Build Community & Drive Repeat Business

With the brand launched, we now focus on turning first-time visitors into loyal regulars and building our online community.

  • Launch User-Generated Content (UGC) Campaign: Create an "Instagrammable" corner in the cafe. Run a monthly contest: "Tag @TheBeanBar.Colombo in your best photo to win a week of free coffee."
  • Introduce Loyalty Program: A simple "Buy 5, get 1 Free" physical or digital stamp card for in-store customers.
  • Micro-Influencer Outreach: Collaborate with 3-5 student/lifestyle micro-influencers from the nearby universities for a contra deal (free meal for posts/stories).
  • Refine Ad Campaigns: Analyze January's ad performance and optimize for conversions (delivery orders) or engagement, focusing on the best-performing audience segments.
